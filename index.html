Nielsen's Heuristics @HVHS

Senior DTEC

This site has lots of information about Nielsen's 10 Heuristics, coined by the Nielsen Norman Group. These are a list of 'rules' that you should follow in order to have good UX design. While most of this information is relevant to the Year 11 DCAT at HVHS, this site is useful to just about anyone. Happy designing!
